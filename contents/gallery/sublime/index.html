Why you

Choose us if appearance, usability, and accessibility are as important to you as to your customers.

Appearance is how a website looks. Are you proud to show it to Mom?

Usability is how a website works. Can Mom use it? Does she love to use it, or is it a pain?

Accessibility is how inclusive a website is. Can Mom's eyesight handle that font?

What makes our conceptions so sublime is that they deliver all three, everywhere. Firefox, Chrome, IE, Opera and Lynx. Mac, Windows, Linux. iPhone, iPad, Blackberry and Android. And plenty of platforms you've never heard of.

Alethia Wist

Developer

Alethia has been slogging through code since she was a teenager. Shortly thereafter, a maurauding band of academics kidnapped her, and held her hostage in an ivory tower. She completed most of a PhD before she escaped.

She speaks Python, JavaScript and PHP. But she prefers English, which is handy for finding out what people are actually, you know, paying her to code.

Alethia is also a strong usability advocate, and has an almost prurient interest in UX testing and other means of figuring out what's going on inside the user's noggin.

She vigorously denies being a cyborg, but admits to occasionally peeking at robots.txt before loading a website.

Alethia's favourite beverage is hot cocoa. Her favourite drink is also hot cocoa, but with rum.

Why ‘conception’

Conception: the moment you conceive of a new idea, a new way of looking at things, a new life. It's the moment something new begins.

 

Why ‘sublime’

Sublime: what is startling, breathtaking, perfect. Take the word apart, you find the latin prefix 'sub-' in front of 'lime', a common citrus fruit.

We're not sure what limes have to do with anything, apart from being a good source of vitamin C, but we're glad we don't have scurvy. So okay then!

Make sure your websites don't get scurvy.

Why us

We produce web sites and apps that use snazzy tech on new machines, but work well on old ones, too. That look just as good on a phone as on a widescreen.

Our promise: if a browser was released in the last ten years, our sites will run on it.

We're committed to human diversity, too. We'll help you get a multilingual site up in the languages of your choice.

And we always make sure our sites aren't just ‘eye sites’. A screenreader test is standard.

Once we make sure that your site reaches everyone, we'll help you climb the ranks on Bing and Google, and we'll leave you with a content style guide that makes it simple to keep your site easy to find and easy on the eyes.

Brooklyn Zelenka

Designer

Brooke started as a classical composer, and found her way into design making extra money doing score layouts. Then she discovered an aptitude for computer composition programming. And a web designer was born!

Her fine arts background serves her well in design. Always interested in pushing the limits within a classic aesthetic, she aims to hit that narrow balance of technique and inspiration.

User experience is something that Brooke takes very seriously. The web is about communicating a message, and every part of a website contributes to its message: from text and images, to layout and navigation.

Brooke's favourite beverage is a traditional cappuccino, served with Beethoven's ninth.
